**Applicability:** Fresh water AND saltwater operation

**Advantages:**

## COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

### Differentiation vs. Market Alternatives

**Traditional Reverse Osmosis (RO) / Ultrafiltration:**
- Higher energy consumption
- More complex membrane maintenance
- Higher capital cost per installation
- Longer setup time

**Advanced Oxidation Processes (AOP):**
- Chemical-dependent
- Higher operational complexity
- Regulatory/logistics burden (chemical supply)

**Simple Filtration Systems (LifeStraw, etc.):**
