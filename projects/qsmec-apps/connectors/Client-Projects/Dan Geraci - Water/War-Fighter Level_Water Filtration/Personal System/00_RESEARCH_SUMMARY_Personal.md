## Risks & Mitigations

- **Filter clogging in high sediment:** Inlet screen + predictive alerts; added spare filters
- **Cold-weather battery derate:** Keep-warm pouch; LiFePO4 chemistry; field SOP
- **Salt scaling:** Rinse protocol; periodic acid wash at depot level
- **Supply chain (UV/microcontrollers):** Dual-source vendors; 2-month buffer stock
