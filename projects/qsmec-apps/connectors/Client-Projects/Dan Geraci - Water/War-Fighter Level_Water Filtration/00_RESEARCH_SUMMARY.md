## COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

### vs. Reverse Osmosis (RO):
- 3-5x lower energy consumption
- Significantly simpler maintenance
- Lower operational costs
- Better scalability

### vs. Ultrafiltration:
- Can remove chemical contaminants (RO can't alone)
- Better cost economics
- Lower maintenance burden

### vs. Simple Filtration (LifeStraw):
- Dual-environment capability (fresh/salt)
- Much broader contaminant range
- Scalable from personal to facility

### Unique Differentiation:
- **Mechanical simplicity** = military logistics advantage
- **Dual-use capability** = broad market appeal
- **Proven technology** = reduced risk
- **QSMEC integration** = "intelligent filtration" positioning
